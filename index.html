Unemployment Around the World

In South Africa, the unemployment rate has never fallen below 20% from 2014 to 2024, reflecting persistent challenges in the labor market. Meanwhile, Cambodia has consistently maintained an unemployment rate below 1%, demonstrating relative stability throughout the same period.

Unemployment and Gender: A Global Divide

Since 2022, Afghanistan's female unemployment rate has surged from 23.82% to 28.30% in 2024, placing it in the top 10 globally. For males, it was not in the top 10 in 2022, but by 2024, it reached 13.09%. Ongoing conflict and political instability have severely impacted the economy, reducing job opportunities and exacerbating unemployment.
